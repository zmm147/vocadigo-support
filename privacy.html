Privacy Policy

Last updated: [2025-5-28]

1. Information We Don't Collect

Vocadigo is designed with your privacy in mind. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. All your media files and learning data are stored locally on your device.

2. Local Storage

The app stores the following data locally on your device:

  • Imported media files (videos, novels, music)
  • Your vocabulary lists and word definitions
  • Learning progress and saved contexts
  • Anki export files (.apkg)

3. Media Access Permissions

Vocadigo requires access to:

  • Local storage - to access your media files
  • Documents folder - to import/export Anki files

4. Third-Party Services

We do not integrate with any third-party services or analytics tools. All functionality is handled locally on your device.

5. Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device, data security depends on your device's security settings. We recommend keeping your device's operating system and Vocadigo app up to date.

6. Children's Privacy

Our app does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under 13.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page.
